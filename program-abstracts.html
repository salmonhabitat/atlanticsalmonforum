Abstract Format Guidelines

  • abstract font: Times New Roman, 12 pt for title, authors and body of text; 10 pt for reference; 400 word maximum (i.e., 1 - single spaced page (inclusive)
  • title: bold-type; scientific names italicized
  • authors: presenting author bold-type; full names; numbered superscripts for affiliations
  • abstract: no paragraph indentation; single-spaced text
  • affiliations: Italicized; superscripts for each author-organization link
  • contact: corresponding author, phone number, and email
