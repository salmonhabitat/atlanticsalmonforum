Registration Information

Online registration for the 2018 Atlantic Salmon Ecosystems Forum will be open between October 1, 2017 through January 12, 2018.

Attendees may also register at the Forum during the morning registration window.

Registration cost is $50 USD for professionals and $25 USD for students.
