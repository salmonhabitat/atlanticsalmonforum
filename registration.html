Registration Information

Online registration for the 2020 Atlantic Salmon Ecosystems Forum will be open between October 1, 2019 through January 1, 2020.

Attendees may also register at the Forum during the morning registration window.

Registration cost is $50 USD for professionals and $25 USD for students.
