Guidelines for Authors

  • Abstract Submissions – DEADLINE DECEMBER 1, 2025
  • All presenters (poster and oral) must register electronically before the meeting
    • Please identify if the submission is for a poster or an oral presentation
    • Please use this abstract example format guidelines
      • abstract font: Times New Roman, 12 pt for title, authors and body of text; 10 pt for reference; 400 word maximum (i.e., 1 - single spaced page (inclusive)
      • title: bold-type; scientific names italicized
      • authors: presenting author bold-type; full names; numbered superscripts for affiliations
      • abstract: no paragraph indentation; single-spaced text
      • affiliations: Italicized; superscripts for each author-organization link
      • contact: corresponding author, phone number, and email
    • Please submit a MS Word file via email here (mark.renkawitz@noaa.gov)
  • Poster Presentation
    • poster size: 48” x 36” maximum
    • easels and pushpins will be provided
  • Oral Presentation
    • Microsoft 2007 OS will be used for the presentation
      • graphics/animation in presentations not prepared with PP07 may not operate properly on the ASEF presentation computer
    • maximum length is 15 minutes and 5 minutes to facilitate Q/A
