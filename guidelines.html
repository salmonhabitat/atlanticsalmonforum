Guidelines for Authors

  • Presenters must register electronically before December 31, 2025
  • Abstracts submission deadline is October 15, 2025
    • FONT: Times New Roman, 12 point
    • TITLE: bold-type; scientific names italicized
    • AUTHORS: full names (First Middle Last); presenting author in bold-type; superscripted numbers for affiliations
    • AFFILIATIONS: Italicized; superscripted number corresponding to each author
    • ABSTRACT BODY: no indentation; single-spaced text; no references/citations; 400 word maximum
    • CONTACT: list corresponding author, phone number and email
    • View an EXAMPLE ABSTRACT (PDF)
    • SUBMIT AN ABSTRACT
  • Oral Presentation
    • Standard AFS Format
    • Target length 15 minutes (firm) including Q/A and Transition
    • Microsoft Windows and Office will be used for the presentation
  • Poster Presentation
    • Poster size: 48” x 36” maximum
    • Easels and pushpins will be provided
