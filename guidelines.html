Guidelines for Authors

  • Presenters must register electronically before January 1, 2018
  • Abstracts submission deadline is December 1, 2017
    • FONT: Times New Roman, 12 point
    • TITLE: bold-type; scientific names italicized
    • AUTHORS: full names (Last Name, First.Middle. Initials); presenting author in bold-type; superscripted numbers for affiliations
    • AFFILIATIONS: Italicized; superscripted number corresponding to each authors
    • ABSTRACT BODY: no indentation; single-spaced text; no references/citations; 400 word maximum
    • CONTACT: list corresponding author, phone number and/or email
    • View an EXAMPLE ABSTRACT (PDF)
    • SUBMIT AN ABSTRACT
  • Oral Presentation
    • Target length 20 min including Q/A and Discussion
    • Microsoft Windows 7 and Office 2010 will be used for the presentation *
  • Poster Presentation
    • Poster size: 48” x 36” maximum
    • Easels and pushpins will be provided

* Please note: presentations with animations and those not prepared with PP07 may not operate properly on the ASEF presentation computer.
